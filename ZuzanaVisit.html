Drug Information from US and EU Regulatory Sites

FDA

DailyMed: US National Library of Medicine's website containing product labeling for drugs approved in the US

Drugs@FDA: FDA warehouse containing labels and SBAs

Europe

EMC: UK's official storage of prescribing and patient information (SmPC's and PILs)

EMA Medicines Search Page: EPARs for drugs under EMA purview can be searched here

EMA Medicines Data: Links to various reports providing drug info

Malta Medicines Authority: Easy access to SmPC's and PILs

INFOlinks: Site has many deadlinks but allows access to SmPC's of many EU countries

Other

DIA presentation: "Harmonization of Labeling Across Products with Same Products with Same Active Moiety in the EU" by Barbara Lachmann, Merck, Oct 2011

Poster Presentation: "Comparison of the US Package Insert and the EU Summary of Product Characteristics" by Bordoloi et al, Rutgers Univ, 2012
